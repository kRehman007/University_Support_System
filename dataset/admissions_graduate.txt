University of Sargodha - Graduate Admissions

Eligibility:
Master's:
- Bachelor's degree, CGPA ≥ 2.5
- GAT General ≥ 50%
- MBA: 2 years work experience preferred

PhD:
- Master's degree, CGPA ≥ 3.0
- GAT Subject ≥ 60%
- Research proposal, references, interview

Requirements:
- Graduate application form
- Transcripts, degree certificates
- Test scores, SOP, CV/Resume
- Reference letters
- Research proposal (PhD), published papers if any

Tuition Fees:
- MS Engineering: $3,800
- MS CS: $3,600
- MBA: $4,200
- MS Natural Sciences: $3,200
- MS Social Sciences: $2,800
- PhD all departments: $2,500

Financial Support:
- Research Assistantships: Full tuition + $800/month
- Teaching Assistantships: 50% tuition + $500/month
- Graduate Scholarships: Merit-based, research grants, international collaborations

Deadlines:
- Fall Semester: Apply Feb 1 – May 31, 2025
- Spring Semester: Apply Sep 1 – Nov 30, 2025
