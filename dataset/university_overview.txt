University of Sargodha - Overview

University Name: University of Sargodha
Location:
  Main Campus: 450 Innovation Boulevard, TechCity, State of Newland, 45001
  North Campus: 78 Research Avenue, TechCity, State of Newland, 45003

Vision:
To be a globally recognized institution that empowers students through innovative education, groundbreaking research, and community engagement.

Mission:
Providing high-quality education, advancing knowledge through research, and serving the community through meaningful partnerships.

Type: Private Non-Profit University
Total Departments: 15 across 5 Faculties
Total Students: 11,250 (Undergrad: 8,500, Grad: 2,300, PhD: 450)
Total Faculty: 530 (Full-time: 420, Part-time: 85, Visiting: 25)

University History:
Established in 1985, University of Sargodha has grown from 3 departments to a comprehensive institution with international recognition and over 35,000 alumni worldwide.
