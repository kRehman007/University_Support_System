University of Sargodha - Undergraduate Admissions

Eligibility:
- Completion of 12 years of education or equivalent
- Minimum GPA: 2.5/4.0
- Program-specific subject requirements (Math, Physics, Biology, etc.)

Requirements:
- Online application form
- High school transcripts and diploma
- National ID / passport
- Photos, entrance test scores, certificates, medical fitness, etc.

Tuition Fees (per semester):
- Engineering: $3,200
- Computer Science: $3,000
- Business Administration: $2,800
- Natural Sciences: $2,600
- Social Sciences & Humanities: $2,400
- Health Sciences: $3,400
- Medicine (MBBS): $8,500

Process & Deadlines:
- Applications open: March 1, 2025
- Applications close: June 15, 2025
- Entrance Test: July 5-10, 2025
- Enrollment: August 20-30, 2025
- Classes start: September 5, 2025

Scholarships & Financial Aid:
- Merit-based: Presidential (100%), Dean's Excellence (75%), Merit (50%), Academic Achievement (25%)
- Need-based: Full or partial tuition coverage
- Special: Sports, Arts & Culture, Minority, Women in STEM, Rural Area
